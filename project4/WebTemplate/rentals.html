Tunnel and Fielding Space Reservations

Unleashed Hitting's locations come equiped with 8 batting tunnels (4 baseball pitching machines, 4 softball pitching machines), a massive indoor turfed field for defensive work, and optional equipment such as a Mr. Fungoman. For full information and to make a reservation please visit our contact page here or visit your local Unleashed Hitting location.


Details and Rates
Tunnels There are 8 batting cages that include the use of a pitching machine, balls, nets/screens, tees, and a chair.

The rates are as followed:
  1. 30 minutes: $30 per tunnel
  2. 60 minutes: $50 per tunnel
  3. 1 hour 30 minutes: $75 per tunnel
  4. Discounts available for 3+ tunnel reservations
Fielding Space The fielding space can be rented as half space or full space. Half space is enough room for a short infield practice, throwing, relays, and baserunning. Full space provides a full size field for a full team practice or scrimmage. Space includes use of bases, nets/screens, balls, and fungobats. Mr. Fungoman can be rented for an additional price, a 30 min free use is included when renting full space for 1 hour and 30 minutes or more hours.

Half-Field rates are as followed:
  1. 30 minutes: $50
  2. 60 minutes: $80
  3. 1 hour 30 minutes: $100
Full-Field rates are as followed:
  1. 30 minutes: $100
  2. 60 minutes: $160
  3. 1 hour 30 minutes: $220
Mr. Fungoman rates are as followed:
  1. 30 minutes: $25
  2. 60 minutes: $40
  3. 1 hour 30 minutes: $55
  4. 30 min free use when purchase of full space for 1hr 30 min or more.
