What is Unleashed Hitting?

Bradley Ross ©
Unleashed Hitting is an all inclusive 24/7 softball and baseball training facility. This facility is equipped with 8 batting cages, open turf field for defensive work, and 6 lane pithing tunnels. All cages are equipped with:
  • Pitching Machine(4 Baseball, 4 Softball)
  • Baseballs, 11-inch Softballs, 12-inch Softballs
  • Pitching Machine Balls
  • Protective Screen
  • 2 Tees
  • Turf Batters Box/Plate Areas
  • A Chair
The open turf field for defensive work is equipped with:
  • 100 Baseballs
  • 60 12-inch Softballs
  • 40 11-inch Softballs
  • 50 Tennis Balls
  • 12 Paddles
  • 2 Fungo Bats
  • Mr. Fungoman*
  • Several Protective Screens/Sock Nets
